1. Purposes of Processing and Categories of Data

1) Purposes

  • Providing and operating the Service: running puzzle stages, saving progress and scores, applying options and settings, and enabling basic gameplay features.
  • Displaying and managing advertisements: showing banner, interstitial and rewarded ads through Google AdMob, limiting frequency, preventing abuse and producing basic statistics.
  • Handling in-app purchases: processing paid items such as coin packs and ad-removal (NO ADS) products, verifying purchases and (where applicable) restoring them.
  • Maintaining app stability: using error and crash logs to improve quality and security.

2) Data that may be processed (mainly via third-party SDKs)

The Company does not ask you to provide, nor does it store on its own servers, direct identifiers such as your name, email address or phone number. The following types of information may be processed automatically by third-party SDKs such as those provided by Google, while the Company has access only to aggregated statistics or dashboards.

  • Device and app information: advertising ID (e.g. AAID), device model, OS version, app version, language and country settings, screen size and similar technical data.
  • Approximate location information: country/region inferred from IP address, for purposes such as ad performance measurement and fraud prevention.
  • Usage data: stage progress, scores, play time, button clicks, ad impressions and other basic usage events.
  • Purchase information: product identifier (SKU), purchase time, Google Play purchase token/receipt, transaction status (success/cancel/refund, etc.).
    Payment instrument details such as card numbers are handled by Google Play Billing, and the Company has no access to them.
  • Diagnostic information: crash logs, error codes and other minimal data related to app stability.

3) Methods of collection

The above information is primarily processed automatically by third-party SDKs (such as Google AdMob and Google Play Billing) when you launch and use the app. The Company may view the results only through the dashboards and tools provided by those third parties, within the scope necessary to operate the Service.

2. Third-Party Processing and Transfers

The Company does not sell your personal data. To provide the Service, the Company entrusts certain processing activities to the following third-party services. Their own terms and privacy policies apply first to how they process and store data.

  • Google AdMob (Google LLC): provides banner, interstitial and rewarded ads, measures ad performance and prevents invalid traffic.
  • Google UMP (User Messaging Platform) SDK: obtains and manages consent for ad-related processing in regions such as the EEA and the UK.
  • Google Play Billing (Google LLC): processes in-app purchases for items such as coin packs and ad-removal products, verifies receipts and handles refunds.

These providers may process and store data in countries outside your own (for example, the United States). The details of such transfers and security measures are governed by each provider’s own privacy policy and terms.
